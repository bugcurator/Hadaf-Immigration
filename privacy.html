Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Hadaf Immigration Consultancy collects, uses, and protects your personal data. Our goal is to maintain transparency, safeguard your privacy, and comply with applicable data protection laws and professional ethics.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only essential data needed for consultation and communication purposes. This includes both personal and non-personal information voluntarily provided by users during interactions or submissions.

  • Full name and contact information (email, phone number).
  • Educational records and documents submitted for processing.
  • Messages or inquiries sent through online forms.
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use the information collected strictly for operational and communication needs related to consultancy services and client assistance.

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested information.
  • Processing visa or admission-related applications.
  • Improving our website and service experience.
  • Maintaining official records for compliance and follow-up.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Your data is processed only under lawful conditions as required by international privacy laws and local regulations. These legal bases include:

  • Consent provided by the user during submission.
  • Performance of contractual or pre-contractual obligations.
  • Legitimate interest in delivering professional consultancy.
  • Compliance with legal or governmental requirements.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Once retention is no longer necessary, information is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Hadaf Immigration Consultancy respects your confidentiality. Information may only be shared in limited, lawful situations such as:

  • With educational partners or embassy officials for application purposes.
  • With legal authorities if required by court order or regulation.
  • With verified third-party service providers bound by confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Security

We implement advanced security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or loss of personal data. Our systems use encryption, secure servers, and restricted staff access to protect sensitive client information.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve performance and monitor visitor preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function correctly without them.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools for analytics, form submissions, or email automation. These partners process limited data under strict data protection standards and cannot use your information for unrelated purposes.

9. International Transfers

If your data is transferred across borders for processing, we ensure such transfers meet international standards, maintaining lawful agreements and ensuring confidentiality and adequate protection at all times.

10. User Rights

You have the right to manage your personal information at any time. These rights include:

  • Requesting a copy of your stored data.
  • Requesting correction or deletion of inaccurate data.
  • Objecting to certain processing activities.
  • Withdrawing consent without affecting prior processing.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Any discovered child-related information will be permanently deleted upon identification.

12. Marketing and Communication

We may occasionally send updates or offers relevant to your inquiries. You can opt out of these communications anytime through unsubscribe options or by contacting us directly via official email.

13. Policy Updates

This policy is reviewed periodically to align with new regulations and practices. Updated versions will be published on our website with an adjusted “Last Updated” date at the top of this document.

14. Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We advise reviewing each third-party privacy policy before submitting personal data.

15. Contact Information

For questions or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at info@hadafimmigration.com. We respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly and transparently.

16. Consent

By using this website or submitting personal information, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, processing, and use of your data as described herein.
