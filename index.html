Who We Are

Guiding Dreams Beyond Borders

Hadaf Immigration Consultancy, founded by Ubaid Ahmad, is dedicated to providing transparent and comprehensive guidance for students aspiring to pursue education in Europe. Our core expertise lies in student placement and visa consultancy, particularly for countries like Lithuania, Poland, and Hungary.

With 5+ years of dedicated experience, we ensure a smooth transition from application to enrollment. We offer end-to-end support, including pre-departure guidance and crucial post-arrival assistance, making your international study journey seamless and stress-free.
